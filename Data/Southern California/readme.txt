Southern California Earthquake Catalog (SCEDC)
=============================================

Source:
  SCEDC (2013): Southern California Earthquake Center. Caltech.Dataset. doi:10.7909/C3WD3xH1.

Time span:
  1981-01-01 00:00:00 to 2022-03-31 23:59:59

Region:
  Latitude between 32 and 37° N,
  Longitude between −121 and −114° E,
  Depth between 0 and 55 km.

Format:
  ElapsedTime(s), Latitude(°), Longitude(°), Magnitude

Fields:
  ElapsedTime(s) — seconds since 1981-01-01 00:00:00
  Latitude, Longitude — Degrees
  Magnitude — Best available magnitude estimate
